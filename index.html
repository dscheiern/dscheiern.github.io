Mathematical Modeling Final Project
Optimizing MLB Pitching Using Markov Chains
Topic: Markov Chains, Transition Matrices, Optimization
View  Transition Matrix  and   final write-up
  • For the first part of my final project, I tried to solve my favorite MLB team's pitching problems.
  • I used a Markov Chain with two absorbing states, and tried to optimize the probability of being absorbed at "Win."
  • For the transition probabilities, I used the concept of predator-prey models to determine which pitching statistics should "help" or "harm" the probability of transitioning to that state.
  • For the second part of my final project, I proved the theorem that the k-step transition matrix of a Markov Chain is the original transition matrix raised to the kth power.
